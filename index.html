CRACKDOWN: A PAYDAY 2 Custom Difficulty

I am the lead programmer and one of two project managers for the Crackdown PAYDAY 2 difficulty. The mod seeks to create a custom difficulty for PAYDAY 2 from scratch while also implementing a custom Special unit, the Grenadier. The mod also comes with adjustments to some core AI behaviors, reworks of various vanilla special units, and a finely tuned assault wave configuration using knowledge gained from reverse-engineering the Group AI code.
