CRACKDOWN: A PAYDAY 2 Custom Difficulty

I am the lead programmer and one of two project managers for the Crackdown PAYDAY 2 difficulty. The mod seeks to create a custom difficulty for PAYDAY 2 from scratch while also implementing a custom Special unit, the Grenadier. The mod also comes with adjustments to some core AI behaviors, reworks of various vanilla special units, and a finely tuned assault wave configuration using knowledge gained from reverse-engineering the Group AI code.

TESLA ENGINE: Space Station 13 Project

I proposed this feature to /tg/station as a lag reduction method. At the time, we were having serious issues with the previous power supply(a gigantic black hole that eats everything and emits radiation) causing severe lag upon being unleashed upon the station. This was proposed as the alternative power supply and was quickly adopted and implemented into a variety of maps as the standard, until the Supermatter power supply was developed later on.

PAYDAY 2: Voiceline Framework

With Restoration Mod and Crackdown both having need for a good voice acting implementation framework to give the Grenadier a voice, a unified framework mod was created to avoid code duplication and ensure anyone can easily add custom voice acting to any unit using SuperBLT's XAudio integration.
